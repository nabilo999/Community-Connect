# Community Connect 

## Purpose 
The purpose of Community Connect is to reignite the idea of "third places" or more commonly known as social enivironments outside of home (first place) and work (second place). Users can sign up with their own account, post and interact with others on events happening around right in their neighborhood.

## Core Features
1. Authentication & Account Sign Up 
2. Dashboard & Feed
3. Groups
4. Events
5. Posts, Discussions, and Polls
6. Messaging Feature 
6. Setting and Profile Customization
